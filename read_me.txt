This repo consists of all the code used to create the results of the study "Comparing connection patterns of innovative companies in hyperlink and social media networks" by Dorian Arifi, Bernd Resch, Jan Kinne and David Lenz.
The utilized data must not be shared due to privacy and commercial reason.

1.)_Preprocessing:
	1.1)_data_scrapping_cleaning
		follower_pipeline_final.py
		mentions_scrapper.py
	1.2)_data_quality_analysis
		difference_in_conections.R
		NPM-tests.R
	1.3)_create_networks
		graph_creation_final.ipynb

2.)_Methodology:
	2.1)_RQ1:
		RQ1_density_clustering_isolates.ipynb
	2.2)_RQ2:
		RQ2_degree_closeness_betweeness.ipynb
		Statistical_tests.R
	2.3)_RQ3:
		RQ3_relational_proximities.ipynb
	2.4)_RQ4:
		Linear_regression_final.R
		Logistic_regression_final.R
